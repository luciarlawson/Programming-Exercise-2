Information about me

In my personal time, I am very passionate about a variety of sports and activities. One of my main interests is volleyball, which I’ve been playing for around three years. This interest continues to fuel my passion for sport and need to stay active. It not only offers a sense of personal fulfilment but also teaches me patience, discipline, and leadership.

Another interest I have is in general crafting projects. I often dedicate weekends or evenings to random "mini projects", as it gives me a chance to disconnect from the hustle and connect with something meaningful. Through this hobby, I've developed my creative skills and learned how to use what I have even if it isn't perfect for the job. I spent a lot of time "crafting" with paracord making things like bracelets, keychains, belts and lanyards. I also spent an evening learning how to crochet an octopus. One of my bigger projects was making an incubator and hatching quail eggs from it. I have also started a scrapbook of places I have visited and learned how to make flowers from pipe cleaners among other things.

I also enjoy kayaking and calisthenics which both keep me physically active and continuously learning. I’m was fascinated by learning how to do a roll in a kayak when I first joined the club. Learning how to was definitely a great way to challenge myself. Calisthenics challenges me more mentally as I have to fight against my body in order to complete the exercises.

Beyond those core interests, I make time for other things like walking, bike rides, skimboarding, surfing, swimming, diving, and general sea bases activities. I find these activities to be both relaxing and rewarding. They allow me to explore new perspectives, appreciate the world around me, and find joy in the little things.
